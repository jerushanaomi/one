A walk to remember

"A Walk to Remember" is a touching tale of love and redemption set in a small town. It follows the transformative journey of Landon and Jamie, two teenagers whose lives intertwine in unexpected ways. Through their profound connection, they discover the power of forgiveness, faith, and the enduring beauty of love.

Percy Jackson Series

"Percy Jackson" is a captivating series written by Rick Riordan, centered around a young demigod named Percy Jackson. With a unique blend of Greek mythology and modern-day adventure, Percy embarks on thrilling quests, battles mythical creatures, and discovers his true identity as the son of Poseidon. Through his bravery and wit, Percy navigates a world filled with gods, monsters, and the challenges of being a hero.

Hunger Games

"The Hunger Games" is a gripping dystopian trilogy written by Suzanne Collins. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, it follows Katniss Everdeen as she volunteers for the annual Hunger Games, a brutal televised event where teenagers fight to the death. Katniss becomes a symbol of rebellion and fights for survival while challenging the oppressive Capitol's control.
